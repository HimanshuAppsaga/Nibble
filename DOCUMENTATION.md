# Nibble — Food Delivery & Ordering App

**A comprehensive, user-centric food ordering and delivery application.**

This application serves as a robust platform for users to discover restaurants, order food for delivery, pickup, or dine-in, and manage their orders seamlessly. It provides an intuitive interface tailored for speed and convenience.

---

## Core Features & Workflow

### 1. Home / Discovery Interface

![Home Interface](Screenshot%202026-07-20%20154414.png)

Users can quickly choose their preferred dining method: **Delivery**, **Pick up**, or **Dine in**. The home screen offers personalized restaurant recommendations, sortable by Recent, Rating, Speed, Price, and Favorites, ensuring users find exactly what they are craving.

### 2. Search & Filtering

![Search Interface](Screenshot%202026-07-20%20154426.png)

A powerful search engine allows users to find specific cravings instantly. Users can toggle between searching for **Restaurants** or specific **Dishes**, with quick-glance information on ratings, delivery time estimates, and distance.

### 3. Order Tracking & History

![Orders Interface](Screenshot%202026-07-20%20154440.png)

A dedicated hub for all transactions. Users can track the real-time status and estimated arrival time of their **Upcoming** orders. The **Past Orders** section provides a detailed log of completed transactions, with options to view details or get help for previous purchases.

### 4. Profile & Account Settings

![Profile Interface](Screenshot%202026-07-20%20154451.png)

Manage user profiles and application settings in one place. Users can access their digital Wallet, view Notifications, manage Favorites, and invite friends to the platform.
